# SmartBrief: State Sportsbook Promos Roundup

## Content Type
**Promotional Roundup / Comparison Guide**

## Target Audience
Sports bettors in a specific state looking for the best welcome bonuses and sportsbook promotions

## Article Structure

### 1. Headline Format
```
Best [STATE] Sportsbook Promos: Claim $[TOTAL_AMOUNT]+ in [STATE_ABBREV] Sports Betting Promos ([DATE])
```

### 2. Introduction (2-3 sentences)
- Lead with the total value available across all promos
- Mention that these are welcome bonuses for new users
- Include a call-to-action to claim the promos
- Note if promos are available in other states as well

### 3. Comparison Table
Create a comprehensive table with the following columns:
- **Sportsbook Name** (with emoji icon)
- **Welcome Bonus** (clear, concise description)
- **Promo Code** (or "BET NOW" link)

**Table Requirements:**
- Include 6-8 major sportsbooks
- Order by value/attractiveness of offer
- Use consistent formatting for bonus descriptions
- Include actual clickable links (not placeholders)

**Typical Sportsbooks to Include:**
1. Fanatics Sportsbook
2. Caesars Sportsbook
3. BetMGM
4. DraftKings
5. FanDuel
6. BetRivers
7. theScore Bet (if available in state)
8. ESPN Bet (if available in state)

### 4. Detailed Promo Breakdowns
For each sportsbook, create a dedicated section with:

#### [Sportsbook Name]: [Bonus Headline]

**Structure:**
- **Opening sentence**: Hook that highlights the unique value proposition
- **How it works**: Clear explanation of the mechanics (deposit requirements, bet requirements, bonus structure)
- **Key terms**: Any important conditions (odds requirements, win/loss conditions, timeframes)
- **Personal touch**: Brief editorial comment about why this offer stands out

**Writing Style:**
- Conversational and enthusiastic
- Focus on value and ease of claiming
- Use phrases like "I'm a big fan of..." or "What's great about this deal is..."
- Highlight what makes each offer unique

Include brief author credentials focusing on:
- Expertise in sports betting industry

- Specialization areas
- Personal sports interests (optional, adds authenticity)

## Writing Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Enthusiastic but trustworthy**: Excited about deals without being pushy
- **Informative**: Clear explanations of complex bonus structures
- **Conversational**: Use "you" and "your" to address reader directly
- **Expert but accessible**: Industry knowledge without jargon overload

### Key Phrases to Use
- "New users can claim..."
- "Just sign up and..."
- "What's great about this deal is..."
- "I'm a big fan of..."
- "All you have to do is..."
- "Note that..."
- "Doing so will get you..."

### SEO Considerations
- Include state name and abbreviation throughout
- Mention specific sportsbook names frequently
- Use variations: "Michigan sportsbook promos", "MI sports betting promos", "Michigan betting bonus"
- Include date in headline for freshness
- Reference popular local teams when relevant

## Content Requirements

### Must Include
- Total dollar value in headline (sum of all bonuses)
- Clear promo codes or "BET NOW" CTAs
- Age and location requirements (21+, state-specific)
- Payment method examples
- Comparison table for quick scanning
- Step-by-step claiming instructions

### Must NOT Include
- Gambling addiction warnings (these are typically added automatically)
- Outdated information or expired promos
- Misleading bonus values
- Promises of guaranteed wins

## AI Generation Instructions

When creating this content:

1. **Research Current Offers**: Verify all bonus amounts and promo codes are current as of the publication date
2. **Calculate Total Value**: Sum all maximum bonus values for the headline
3. **Customize for State**: Replace all [STATE] and [STATE_ABBREV] placeholders with the target state
4. **Maintain Consistency**: Ensure all sportsbook names are spelled correctly and consistently
5. **Verify Links**: All "BET NOW" links should be functional (or marked as placeholder)
6. **Check Compliance**: Ensure all terms accurately reflect the sportsbook's actual terms and conditions
7. **Add Local Flavor**: Reference local sports teams or events when relevant (e.g., "go toward a new Lions jersey" for Michigan)
8. **Update Date**: Use current date in headline and throughout content

## Example Bonus Descriptions

### First Bet Insurance Style
"New players who sign up and place a first bet of up to $[AMOUNT] will have that bet matched in the same amount in the form of a single bonus bet. I'm a big fan of this [STATE] sportsbook promo as it doesn't carry the requirement that you need to win or lose your first bet."

### Bet & Get (Win Required) Style
"Just sign up and place any $[SMALL_AMOUNT] first wager to earn $[LARGE_AMOUNT] in bonus bets if that bet wins. Note that you'll only unlock that bonus if your bet wins, so find a lock and the $[LARGE_AMOUNT] is yours!"

### Bet & Get (Guaranteed) Style
"New bettors up to $[AMOUNT] in [CURRENCY/BONUS TYPE] just by betting up to $[DAILY_AMOUNT] each day for [X] days. All you have to do is sign up as a new user and place a $1+ first bet each day to earn that same amount in [CURRENCY/BONUS TYPE] each of those days."

### Second Chance Bet Style
"[Sportsbook] offers a great no-sweat bet as a welcome bonus. With the [Sportsbook] bonus code, new users can place a first bet of up to $[AMOUNT], and if that first bet loses, [Sportsbook] will reimburse that loss in bonus bets."
